## Key Terms/Formulas

$$
\begin{aligned}
    w_j &:= w_j + \Delta w_j \\
    b &:= b + \Delta b \\
    \Delta w_j &= \eta(y^{(i)} - \hat{y}^{(i)})x_j^{(i)} \\
    \Delta b &= \eta(y^{(i)} - \hat{y}^{(i)})
\end{aligned}
$$
